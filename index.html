Our Process
1. Product Ideation
By deep-diving into discustions on product and target users, we work together to understand what you are building and who you are building for
2. Product Design
We break down product features into user flow diagrams, building on this with a product flow map and key screen wireframes for prototype testing. Then we work together to determine UI style and create UI design across all screens
3. Iterative Development
The development team implements product features in rapid 2-week iterations, focusing on the most well-defined and highest priority stories in the product backlog
